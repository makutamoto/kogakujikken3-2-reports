$R=1 \mathrm{M}$の時、
$$18\frac{R_{xy}}{1 M + R_{xy}} = 9.2 V$$
$$18R_{xy} = 9.2 M + 9.2 R_{xy}$$
$$8.8R_{xy} = 9.2M$$
$$R_{xy} = 1.05 \mathrm{M\Omega}$$

$R=500 \mathrm{k}$の時、
$$18\frac{R_{xy}}{500 k + R_{xy}} = 12.2 V$$
$$18R_{xy} = 6.1 M + 12.2 R_{xy}$$
$$5.8R_{xy} = 6.1 M$$
$$R_{xy} = 1.05 \mathrm{M\Omega}$$

よって、
$$R_{xy} = 1.05 \mathrm{M\Omega}$$
